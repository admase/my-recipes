Kalua Pig/Pork

Description

Kalua pig (also called kalua pork) is a popular Hawaiian dish you can easily make at home in the oven or with an Instant Pot (or any pressure cooker).

Incredients
  • 3 pounds boneless pork butt, cut into 3 pieces
  • 1 tablespoon Hawaiian sea salt (or regular sea salt)
  • 1 tablespoon liquid smoke
  • 1 cup water
Steps: To prepare Kalua Pork in the oven
  1. Score the pork butt all over and season with sea salt and liquid smoke.
  2. Place it in a roasting pan and cover tightly with foil.
  3. Roast 3-4 hours at 325F, until the meat is really tender.
  4. Let cool a little and then shred the pork meat, adding back some of the kalua pork juices/drippings to make it extra juicy. Eat with rice.
